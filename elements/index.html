    ISO/IEC 27001:2022 Information Security Management System Schema

    A comprehensive LinkML schema modeling the requirements, clauses, and Annex A controls of ISO/IEC 27001:2022. Designed for open data publication, automated validation, and integration with governance, risk, and compliance (GRC) systems. This schema captures: - ISMS lifecycle (establish, implement, maintain, improve) - Risk assessment and treatment processes (Clause 6.1) - All 93 Annex A controls organized by domain - Audit, measurement, and continual improvement artifacts

    URI: https://w3id.org/lmodel/iso-iec-27001

    Name: iso27001
